About the Event

Get Ready for the Ultimate Tug of War Challenge! The Rassi Khech Competition 2025 is back — bigger, louder, and more thrilling than ever! Teams from across Maharashtra will clash in a battle of strength, skill, and teamwork to claim the ultimate championship title! Hosted in the vibrant village of Basarge, known for its rich traditions and festive spirit, this annual event brings together people of all ages for a day packed with energy, entertainment, and excitement. Gather your team, grip the rope, and feel the adrenaline rush as the crowd cheers you on! Join us for an unforgettable celebration of power, unity, and sportsmanship — with exciting prizes waiting for the champions!

Rules & Regulations

1)Each team must consist of 5 players.

2) All teams must report on time.

3) Once registration is done, no changes in team members will be allowed.

4️)Organizers will not be responsible for any injuries caused during the competition.

5️) Each team will get only one chance, and a player can participate only once and for a single team.

6️) Other rules and details will be announced at the competition venue.
